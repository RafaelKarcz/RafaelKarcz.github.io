Intro

Welcome to my corner of the digital world.
I’m Rafael Karcz. I'm a Data & Business Analyst and Definite Optimist who believes that small shifts can create deep impact.

With a background in International Management & Business Economics and a certification as a Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst (Coursera), I specialize in turning data into decisions and building workflows that save time, reduce friction, and open space for growth.

My toolkit spans Power BI (DAX, star-schema modeling, deployment pipelines, RLS, governance), SQL (CTEs, window functions, optimization), and Python (automation scripts, data wrangling, web scraping), along with Git, Bash, and REST APIs. In the travel tech industry, I’ve applied these skills to optimize CRM systems for 800+ users, support database stability, and contribute to automation projects that keep complex operations running smoothly.

But beyond analytics, I’m an adventurer at heart. From the bustling streets of Central America to the serene landscapes of New Zealand and the cultural depth of Japan, travel has shaped how I see the world: adaptable, curious, and open to diverse perspectives.

At the intersection of data, technology, and global experience, I carry a simple ethos:

Let the good travel.
Whether it’s insights, ideas, or connections, I believe that the right things should move freely, make a difference, and leave a positive mark.

If this resonates with you, let's connect — over data, collaboration, or even just a good coffee.
